# Learn w. LA: Marketing Thesis Training

A small learning space for Marketing English practice.

This project was created to help students learn marketing vocabulary, understand key concepts, and practice speaking with more confidence.

## Live site

Open the learning page here:

https://learnwithla.github.io/marketing-thesis-training/

## What is inside?

This site has two main parts:

### 1. Flip Cards for Vocabs

Students can review marketing words one by one.

Each card shows:

* the English word
* a short Vietnamese translation
* a simple English explanation

Click the card to reveal the meaning.

### 2. Practice Time

Students can practice example sentences with blanks.

Click the blank to reveal the correct answer.

This helps students remember the word in context, not just memorize the definition.

## Who is this for?

This site is made for students who want to improve:

* Marketing English vocabulary
* thesis presentation skills
* speaking confidence
* quick English response
* basic marketing thinking

## Why I made this

Learning marketing in English can feel difficult at first.

Sometimes students know the idea in Vietnamese, but they cannot explain it clearly in English yet.

This tool gives them a simple way to review, practice, and speak more naturally step by step.

## How to use it

1. Start with the vocabulary cards.
2. Say each word out loud.
3. Try to explain the meaning in your own words.
4. Go to Practice Time.
5. Fill in the blank before clicking the answer.
6. Repeat the difficult words again.

Small practice every day is better than memorizing everything at once.
